## Steps to Publish

### 1. **Access the Bounty Form**
   - Go to your **Profile/Dashboard**
   - Navigate to the **"Bounties"** section
   - Click **"Post a New Bounty"**

### 2. **Fill Required Information**
   - **Title**: Clear, descriptive project name
   - **Category**: Choose from Contents, Code, or Others
   - **Budget**: Set amount and currency (USD, USDC, ETH, or BTC)
   - **Description**: Explain the problem, context, and goals
   - **Deliverables**: List what you expect (PR, demo, docs, etc.)

### 3. **Add Optional Details**
   - **Deadline**: Set submission deadline (optional)
   - **Repository URL**: Link to GitHub repo or reference site
   - **Acceptance Criteria**: Define how you'll evaluate work
   - **Payment Method**: On-chain (crypto) or Off-chain (fiat)
   - **Bounty Type**: Solo Winner or Multiple Winners

### 4. **Submit & Review**
   - Click **"Submit Bounty"**
   - Your bounty goes live immediately
   - Edit anytime after creation

## How It Works

1. **Your bounty appears** on the Opportunities page
2. **Talented contributors** browse and submit proposals
3. **You review submissions** and select winners
4. **Payment is processed** based on your chosen method
5. **Work is delivered** according to your acceptance criteria
